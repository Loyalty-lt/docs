## Security Considerations

<Warning>
QR sessions expire after 5 minutes for enhanced security. Always implement proper session management and real-time status monitoring.
</Warning>

## Real-time Integration

This endpoint works in conjunction with Ably WebSocket communication for real-time status updates. Subscribe to the `qr-login:{sessionId}` channel to receive authentication events.
